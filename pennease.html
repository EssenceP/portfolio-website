PennEase

PennEase is a money saving app created with a simplistic and trustworthy interface. The design process for this app included establishing user stories and a user flow diagram reflecting the goal of assisting users in their savings goals, frequently visiting the empathize, define, and ideate stages in the Design Thinking Method, and receiving user feedback to determine if the needs of the user highlighted in the user stories were successfully met during the prototype and testing stages.
